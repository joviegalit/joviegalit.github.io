Jovie Marie Galit

I use my own experience of oppression as a source of strength and inspiration to be a part of Social Service Work. It provides me a sense of self-awareness and a will to strive for a fair-minded attitude in the field. It also gives me a sense of purpose and a motivation to build solidarity with individuals who face similar issues - all while being determined to infuse a change in the society.

I am a highly motivated professional with over 3 years of experience in social service agencies, demonstrating aptitude in: program coordination, volunteer management, administration, group facilitation, and community development. I have established excellent needs assessment and resource referral skills through years of experience in front line client support positions. I also demonstrate extensive knowledge of social diversity and equality issues, as well as the ability to establish an anti-oppressive approach throughout all aspects of practice.

I am highly adept at working with a diverse clientele, including: newcomers, youth, children, individuals with mental disabilities, families, and seniors.
